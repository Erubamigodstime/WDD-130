Courses We Offer

Facility Management professional Courses

Facility Management Master Craft

The Facility management master craft diploma: is for anyone engaged in, interested in Facility Management, or managing a built environment generally. The training is aimed at equipping practicing/professional Facility Managers with up-to-date industry knowledge and technical skills, tools, and templates.

Facility Management Business Master class

The Facility Management Master Class is for facility and property managers, operations management professionals, directors and managers. it is designed to deliver cutting-edge skills, tools and templates for best in class Facility Management.

Facility Management Strategy

The facility management strategy course is for facilities, real estates and property departmental leaders, financial, procurement, general services and administrative heads who oversee budgets, strategic planning, and procurement of FM services. This course will treat various FM contracting models, value-based FM contracting processes and as well as FM organization and leadership.
