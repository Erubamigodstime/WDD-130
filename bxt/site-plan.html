Overview

Purpose

one of the economic problems faced in the world today is a high rate of unemployment especially among youth, and this is not just because of the unavailability of Jobs but graduates and individual not skilled enough for set roles. At BXT Academy Our goal with the website is to upskill the general public/ job seekers and make them qualified for whatever role in variety of field, to create a learning platform for those that are employed to improve and transform their career through series of comprehensive trainings and skills acquisition in variety of field. a well-recognized certificate will be issued. Would you want to remain stagnant in knowledge in this world that is transforming rapidly with innovations, technology and much more? The website is also aimed to create entrepreneurs out of our youth and make them self-employed rather than mere job seekers. and this we are certain we can achieve because vast knowledge, learning tools and years of experience among the c suites, executives and labor market.

Audience

My First target is the youth, as the old saying goes that they are the leaders of tomorrow, People in the labor market especially those in search of jobs, people trying to transit from one occupation to another especially relating to the built environment like Facility managers Project managers, Civil Engineers, Electrical, all building construction Engineers. The website is also aimed to target the talented middle-class individuals that might not be financially buoyant enough to get good education and individuals who would want to transform their career.
